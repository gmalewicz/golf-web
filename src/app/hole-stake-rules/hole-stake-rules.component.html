Game rules

Game is available for 3 or 4 players with 3 possible stakes.

The rule is very simple: The winner is the player who has the best score for a given hole.

In case of two (or more winners), the stake is split among them.

No matter who is on the second or third pleace, the stake goes only to the winner.

If all players have the same result, the hole is skipped.


Application functionality

Enter position for each player starting from the winner (select 1), the second (select 2) and so on.

Application will not allow to complete a hole if at least one player is not marked as a winner (select 1).

In case of mistake the hole can be editted by pressing Done button.

The hole will be opened for edition. When completed press Edit button again to finalize it.

The score is displayed at the buttom of the page. It is recalculated after the complition of each hole

or finalization of edition.
